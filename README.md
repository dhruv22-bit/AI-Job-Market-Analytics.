# 📌 Project Overview

The **AI Job Market Analytics Dashboard** is an end-to-end data analytics project that explores hiring trends in the Artificial Intelligence and Data industry.

Using **Python**, **SQL**, and **Power BI**, this project analyzes thousands of AI job postings to uncover hiring patterns, salary trends, skill demand, company insights, and location-based opportunities.

The dashboard helps job seekers, recruiters, and business professionals make data-driven decisions by presenting interactive visualizations and actionable insights.

---

# 🎯 Business Objectives

✅ Identify the most in-demand AI & Data skills

✅ Analyze salary trends across different job roles

✅ Compare Remote, Hybrid, and On-site opportunities

✅ Discover top hiring companies

✅ Find the highest-paying locations

✅ Analyze hiring trends based on experience level

# 📈 Business Key Insights

### 📍 Executive Overview

- Bangalore emerged as the leading AI hiring hub, accounting for the highest number of job postings among all analyzed locations.
- Hybrid work dominates the job market, indicating that organizations increasingly prefer flexible work models over fully remote or on-site roles.

### 💰 Salary Analytics

- AI Engineer roles offer the highest average salaries, while Data Analyst positions provide competitive entry-level opportunities.
- Salary increases significantly with experience, with professionals having 10+ years receiving the highest average compensation.
  
### 🧠 Skills Analytics

- SQL and Python are the most in-demand skills, appearing in the majority of AI and data-related job postings.
- Power BI, Excel, and cloud technologies (Azure/AWS) are highly valued alongside technical programming skills, highlighting the demand for well-rounded professionals.

### 🏢 Company & Market Insights

- A few leading companies account for a significant share of AI job openings, indicating concentrated hiring within the technology sector.
- Mid-level professionals (3–5 years of experience) represent the largest hiring segment, reflecting strong market demand for experienced talent with practical industry skills.
